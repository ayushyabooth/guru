GURU RINGS

Three interlocking efforts converging toward mastery.
Color as light radiating through glass — not painted on top.

A — Luminous Glass Torus
Rings as translucent glass tubes with light radiating from within. Specular rim highlights, caustic overlaps, volumetric nexus.
B — Plasma Flow
Energy flowing through crystalline channels. Hot white core, diffuse aura, particle wake at leading edge. Organic, alive.
C — Frosted Glass Orbital
Thick frosted glass rings with brand colors glowing from beneath. Material honesty — blur declares depth. Ethereal.
D — Neon Filament
Thin luminous filaments with intense bloom halo. Minimal, striking, maximum contrast. Color IS light.
Design Manifesto Applied: Material honesty through layered translucency · Color as light, not pigment · Spatial rhythm of physical stacking · Liquid motion, never mechanical
